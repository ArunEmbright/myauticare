Your Child's Journey

  • Screening / diagnose to better understand your child
  • Free consultation with an expert/ autism consultant
  • Baseline skill assessment and providing report/ data
  • Create Individual Education Plan (IEP)
  • Training parents to administer skills
  • Tracking progress
  • Customized low, mid and high AT tools for supporting therapy as per the guidance of the therapist
  • Monitor skill acquisition by the therapist
  • Behaviour modification

Skill that your child will develop

There are different Skills enhanced and improved using Auticare through therapist guided contents and activity modules.

Scenarios are customisable according to the specific IEP goals and target skills sets of the child.
